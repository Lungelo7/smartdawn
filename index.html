Who We Are?

Smart Dawn Pro Studios is a professional South African Studio showing off talent from all sorts of arts, Music, Dancing, Drawing, Animation etc..

What We Do?

We promote pure talent, from young stars to Adults who are mature and ready to produce, publish their content to make a living.

Why We Do It?

There is a demand of promoters and artist managers in the music industry and there is a need to show the world all other arts in a platform where there is no fear or favor.

Since When?

Smart Dawn Pro Studios was established in the year 2016 and became a registered business on July 2021
